## Best Practices

1. **Personalization is key** - Always mention something specific about them
2. **Lead with value** - Free minutes show confidence in product
3. **Keep it short** - Especially for DMs and first contact
4. **Be specific** - Mention exact use cases relevant to them
5. **Easy next step** - Include code/link for immediate action
6. **Follow up** - But max 2 times, then move on
7. **Track everything** - Data helps refine approach

## A/B Test Variables

Test different versions:
- Subject lines
- Free minutes amount (50 vs 100 vs 200)
- Emphasis (privacy vs convenience vs cost)
- CTA (try it vs demo vs free minutes)
- Length (ultra-short vs detailed)
- Tone (casual vs professional)
